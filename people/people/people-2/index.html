Youjin Jeon
#people

Who are you, where do you live and work?

Hello, I’m Youjin Jeon from Seoul, working as a composer and media artist, and now I’m running a community called WOMAN OPEN TECH LAB.

How would you define mediating media arts / media art mediation?

Our society is kind of obsessed with learning and presenting new, emerging, cutting-edge technologies. Mediation is an attempt to overcome or bypass the many problems and limitations of the existing educational process. It not only helps people build technology literacy, but it also helps them respect their autonomy and form individual perspectives of technology.

What specific issues and concerns do you have in the field of education and technology?

As media arts expands, at the same time, the lack or diversity is seen in many ways. I pay attention to the deepening of existing inequality due to technology. Technology is what makes human life progress, but it also generate problems and discomfort that did not exist. WOMAN OPEN TECH LAB develops pedagogies that encompass the multifacetedness of technologies and bridge the gap.

What food would you bring to our campside?

Any food that we can feel the freshness of nature would be great.
